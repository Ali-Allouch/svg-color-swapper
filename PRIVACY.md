# Privacy Policy for SVG Color Swapper

**Effective Date: April 20, 2026**

At **SVG Color Swapper**, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle data and ensures that your browsing experience remains private and secure.

### 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal information.** SVG Color Swapper is designed to function entirely within your local browser environment. We do not collect, store, or transmit:
* Your browsing history.
* Personal identities (names, emails, etc.).
* Any data related to the websites you visit.

### 2. Permissions
The extension requires permissions to function, and here is how they are used:
* **activeTab & scripting:** Used solely to scan the current webpage for SVG elements and allow you to modify their colors locally. This data never leaves your computer.
* **Storage:** Used only to save your preferences within the extension's local context.

### 3. Data Transmission
**No data is ever sent to a remote server.** All processing, color swapping, and SVG code generation happen locally on your device. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking scripts.

### 4. Third-Party Websites
While the extension interacts with SVGs on third-party websites, it does not share any information with those websites or any other third party.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions, you can open an issue on the [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Ali-Allouch/svg-color-swapper).
